About Austin

Hi there! I'm as tall and awkward looking as they come! I've got red hair, and I'm super easy to spot in a crowd! I love drinking coffee with friends and just talking for hours!

I've loved technology and the internet since I was young. I remember using a computer in elementary school, and be utterly amazed by it. Luckily, that amazement hasn't stopped. Eventually, in college, I realized I wanted to spend the rest of my life working closely with it.

I specialize in modern responsive website design. I don't currently have an office space, but I base my operations out of Marion, Indiana. I work closely with HTML, CSS, and Twitter Bootstrap. I also deal a lot with hosting packages, and have some experience in wordpress. When I do business, I strive to always make my clients and business partners happy!
